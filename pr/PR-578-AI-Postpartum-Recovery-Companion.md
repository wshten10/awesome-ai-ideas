## 🔍 Risk Assessment

### Technical Risks
- **Data Privacy**: Healthcare data requires strict compliance (HIPAA equivalent)
- **Algorithm Accuracy**: AI recommendations need medical validation
- **System Reliability**: Health monitoring requires 99.9% uptime
- **Integration Complexity**: Multiple platform API integrations

### Business Risks
- **Market Acceptance**: New mothers may be skeptical of AI healthcare
- **Competition**: Existing maternal health apps have established user base
- **Regulatory Compliance**: Healthcare app regulations vary by region
- **Revenue Model**: Freemium conversion rates may be lower than expected

### Mitigation Strategies
1. **Partnership Approach**: Collaborate with hospitals and healthcare providers
2. **Expert Validation**: Involve medical professionals in development
3. **Gradual Rollout**: Start with basic features, add complexity gradually
4. **Privacy-First Design**: Local processing where possible, clear data policies
